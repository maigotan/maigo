If you haven't guessed by now, Maigo translates Japanese comics for an English audience.

On July 26th, 2009, Maigo was started under two kids with no direction in life.

Yet over the years, slowly but surely, it grew. Members, policies, inside jokes. A subculture.

Eventually, the two kids stopped being lost.

  • This is not a paid service. Don't expect the timeliness of one.
  • Retranslations are fine, but not recommended. Credits are optional.
  • Bartering is possible.
  • No joints for groups bigger than one member.
